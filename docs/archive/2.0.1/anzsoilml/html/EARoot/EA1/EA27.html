A data type to capture upper and lower depth boundary values.<br /></p><p>Treated as a special range for depths where the "upper" value will be numerically smaller than the "lower" depth as opposed to a (e.g.) "DiscreteQuantityRange" where the upper value will be larger than the lower value.<br /></p><p>Different from the INSPIRE "RangeType" dataType also used for depth values but where the deeper boundary is referred to as the "upperBoundary" (unless -ve values are used for depth).<br /></p>
